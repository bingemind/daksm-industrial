Quality Policy

Committed to Excellence at Every Stage

At DAKSM Engineering Industries, quality is not a department — it is a culture. Our ISO 9001:2015 certified management system governs every stage of the value chain, from raw material procurement and in-process machining to final hydrostatic and pneumatic testing, ensuring zero-defect delivery.

We are committed to continual improvement through data-driven root-cause analysis, rigorous supplier qualification, and calibrated inspection equipment traceable to national standards. Customer satisfaction and regulatory compliance are the twin pillars of every decision we make on the shop floor and beyond.
