About Us

25 Years of
Engineering Excellence

Founded in 2000, DAKSM Engineering Industries has built a reputation as one of India's foremost manufacturers of forged and cast steel valves — supplying gate, globe, check, ball, and butterfly valves for the most demanding applications in chemical, petrochemical, oil & gas, nuclear power, cogeneration, and cryogenic industries.

Every valve is designed, manufactured, and inspected to strictly conform with ASTM, ASME, API, BS, and other internationally recognised standards. Our stainless and high-alloy steel valves are characterised by unsurpassed corrosion resistance and reliability — delivering the lowest cost of ownership and the longest service life in the industry.

As an ISO 9001 certified manufacturer, we combine updated technical know-how with an integrated production system across two specialised plants in Mumbai. Our commitment is to continuously bring innovative valve designs to market, with special emphasis on quality, safety, and ease of operation.
